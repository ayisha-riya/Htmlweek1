INDIA STATES

KERALA

Kerala is a southern Indian state known as "God's Own Country" for its stunning natural beauty, which includes backwaters, beaches, and lush green hills.

DELHI

Delhi is special for its unique blend of rich history and rapid modernization, evident in its numerous ancient monuments and bustling contemporary infrastructure.

RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan is famous for its royal heritage, vibrant culture, and stunning desert landscapes, making it a popular tourist destination.

JAIPUR

Jaipur is special as the "Pink City," a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its unique pink-hued architecture, historic forts, and grand palaces like the Hawa Mahal and Amber Fort.

PUNJAB

Punjab is renowned for its vibrant culture, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality, making it a lively and welcoming destination.Known in English as the Golden Temple.

TAMILNADU

Tamil Nadu, a South Indian state, is famed for its Dravidian-style Hindu temples. In Madurai, Meenakshi Amman Temple has high ‘gopuram’ towers ornamented with colourful figures.
